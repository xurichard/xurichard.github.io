"Move swift as the Wind and closely-formed as the Wood. Attack like the Fire and be still as the Mountain."

"That's when I feel most alive, he told me once on an airplane, when I'm helping people."
- Tracy Kidder talking to Paul Farmer, Mountains Beyond Mountains

Having delt with my own problems by high school, I decide to turn my attention to others after reading Mountains Beyond Mountains by Tracey Kidder. He told the story of Dr.Paul Farmer, who went to restructure Haiti's heathcare system, lower the cost of second line TB drugs, and reform WHO's TB treatment protocalls. I wanted to change the world and help as many people as he had, to invent devices to increase quality of life, to lower drug prices for those in need, to take care of others as I had been. I might not achieve as much as Farmer, but I'll sure as hell try to.

"Come on Richard, one last push." - Coach Hill, Bellevue High School Track Team

During my entire high school career, I've only run one 5k.
I wasn't the fastest.
I wasn't ever in the lead.
I can't say I won in any category.
But I can say that I gave it my all. After hearing these words from my coach in the last 100 meters, I ran faster and pushed with strength I didn't have. I wasn't the best, but coach Hill definitely taught me to work harder, aim higher and strive to be the best. And that's a mindset I continue to have to this day.
